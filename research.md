# Deep Research Agent - Potential Use Cases

This document stores "hot in the market" ideas for future reference or alternative projects.

1.  **Automated Lead Generation (B2B)**
    *   **Trigger**: A WhatsApp message with a company name or industry.
    *   **Workflow**: `Researcher` finds company details & executives. `Analyst` drafts a personalized cold email based on recent news.
2.  **Stock Market & Financial Analyst**
    *   **Trigger**: A Slack slash command (e.g., `/analyze AAPL`).
    *   **Workflow**: `Data Collector` fetches SEC filings/news. `Financial Analyst` summarizes sentiment, risks, and provides a report.
3.  **Venture Capital / Startup Due Diligence**
    *   **Trigger**: Webhook from a CRM or Slack message with a startup's URL.
    *   **Workflow**: `Investigator` deep-dives into founders' backgrounds, market size, and competitor landscape. `Summarizer` outputs an investment memo.
4.  **Competitive Intelligence & Pricing Analyst**
    *   **Trigger**: Scheduled cron job or WhatsApp message with a competitor name.
    *   **Workflow**: `Scraper` monitors competitor pricing, product updates, and marketing campaigns. `Strategist` generates a SWOT analysis.
5.  **Cybersecurity Threat Intel Analyst**
    *   **Trigger**: An email or Slack alert with an IP address, domain, or CVE vulnerability.
    *   **Workflow**: `OSINT Agent` gathers threat reports from multiple sources. `Risk Analyst` summarizes vulnerabilities and recommends mitigations.
6.  **Real Estate Market & Zoning Analyst**
    *   **Trigger**: A WhatsApp message with an address or zip code.
    *   **Workflow**: `Property Researcher` analyzes listings, neighborhood data, and recent sales. `Legal Agent` checks zoning laws and property history.
7.  **Supply Chain Risk Analyzer**
    *   **Trigger**: Slack alert for a specific material or supplier name.
    *   **Workflow**: `Global Monitor` checks weather, geopolitical news, and trade data. `Risk Analyst` assesses potential disruptions for the supply chain.
8.  **Medical Literature Reviewer**
    *   **Trigger**: An email or Slack message with a medical condition or drug name.
    *   **Workflow**: `Medical Researcher` searches PubMed/ArXiv for recent papers. `Summarizer` extracts key findings and clinical trial results.
